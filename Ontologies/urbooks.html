Description

The Under-Represented Books Ontology is an ontology aimed at modelling works created by Western and Transnational writers born from 1808. The ontology relies on FRBR for the encoding of works, expressions, and editions, and on DOLCE for the representations of prizes and publications. The Under-Represented Books Ontology is parte of the Under-Represented Writer project, which is aimed at exploring and mitigating the potential lack of representation of non-Western writers.
